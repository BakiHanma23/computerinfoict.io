Information and Communications Technology Tools

LAPTOP

A laptop is programmable machine designed to store and retrieve information and perform arithmetic and logical operation to produce meaningful result in desired format.

 Smartphone

Smartphone are ICT tools for taking to people. You can make calls and do things that you normally do using a computer such as browsing the web, sending e-mail, listening music and much more.

 iPad

The iPad are useful devices that fulfil the curriculum requirements for ICT. The iPad is a light weight and cost effective solution for students to integrate ICT into each learning area.
